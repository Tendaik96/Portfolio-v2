About me

I'm an aspiring developer who has a degree in Medicinal and Pharmaceutical Chemistry. I began my career as a sterile specialist, where I developed valuable skills in attention to detail and problem-solving. During the Covid-19 pandemic, I pursued my interest in technology and began learning HTML and CSS. This eventually led me to JavaScript and a passion for software development. Seeking to accelerate my learning process, I enrolled in the School of Code, where I completed and graduated as a Full-Stack Developer.


As a Junior Full-Stack Developer, I am excited to collaborate with others, be creative, and use a strategic approach to problem-solving. My goal is to design cutting-edge web applications that provide an exceptional user experience. Although I am still new to this field, I am eager to continue learning and developing my skills.
